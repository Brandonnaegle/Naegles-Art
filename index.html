About

Brandon Naegle is a husband and father from Utah who creates custom art for businesses, individuals, events, and more. While I mostly use an airbrush to create art, I often use other medians as projects require. I have spent my career doing painting and fabrication in multiple industries including: aerospace, construction, and automotive. This has provided me a solid technical background for producing art on many types of surfaces. I use this background to produce high quality and professional art from concept, to design , to execution. Please check out my portfolio for examples of completed art projects and contact me to discuss your upcoming project or event.
